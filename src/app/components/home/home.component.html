City Care

Our goal is simple, connect every community with their local nonprofit organizations.

We want to make your local nonprofit organizations more easily accessible by letting you connect to them. You can see inventory, contacts, and even post your needs for other users and orgs to see! Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for you to receive the care and resources that you need! This will be a point of connection for our users and organizations to better serve one another. We believe this application has the power to enrich communities through helping hands. Thank you for using City Care.
