Four friends all attending Bethel School Of Technology, who took the Full Stack Web Developer Program, came together to solve a real-world need.
Due to the COVID19 Pandemic, communities and people around the country have faced an unprecedented lack of the necessities that most people take for granted.
Because of this pandemic, the team decided that for their final group project, they would focus on those needs. During the project proposal phase, the Lord put it on their hearts to create the City Care App. The product will be an application that puts the resources in the hands of the community and the community in touch with the resources to be able to meet those needs.
