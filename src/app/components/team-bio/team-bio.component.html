About the team

Four friends all attending Bethel School Of Technologies Full Stack Web Developer Program came together to solve a real world need. Due to the Covid19 Pandemic, communities and people around the country have faced an unprecidented lack of the basic necessities that most people take for granted. Because of this, the team decided that for the final project they would focus on those needs. The end product will be an application that puts the resources in the hands of the community and the community in touch with the resources to be able to meet those needs.
